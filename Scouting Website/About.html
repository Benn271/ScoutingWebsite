About

Introduction

Welcome to our Scouting Website, your go-to resource for simplifying scouting in FIRST competitions! We understand the challenges faced by teams when it comes to gathering accurate and comprehensive information about their competitors. That's why we have developed this platform with the sole purpose of making scouting easier, more efficient, and more effective. At our website, you will find a wealth of valuable tools and resources specifically designed to assist scouting teams in their quest for success. Our user-friendly interface ensures that even those new to scouting can navigate through the site effortlessly. We believe that by providing reliable and up-to-date information, we can empower scouting teams to make informed decisions and devise winning strategies. With our commitment to excellence and dedication towards enhancing the scouting experience, we aim to be your trusted partner throughout your FIRST competition journey.

Team

This website was made by Benjy and JD, two team members of team 3179. Each member brings unique expertise to the table, ensuring that our platform meets the specific needs of scouting teams. Our developers employ technologies to design a seamless interface that allows users to efficiently gather and analyze data. The creative minds on our design team ensure that the website is visually appealing while maintaining its functionality. Our team of two will try to send out update the website for each FIRST game.

Communication

We are always here to assist you in making scouting at FIRST competitions effortless. If you have any questions, concerns, or require further information about our website and its functionalities, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. Our dedicated team of experts is readily available to provide you with prompt and reliable assistance.
